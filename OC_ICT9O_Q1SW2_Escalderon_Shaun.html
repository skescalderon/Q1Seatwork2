Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin Juan studied at Spain for 7 years. He also told about the life of Don Rafael.
Maria Clara Maria is the wife of Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin. She was raised= by Tiya Isabel.
Father Damaso Father Damaso is father of Maria Clara. Father Damaso represents corrupt and hungry of power in the story.
Tiya Isabel Isabel raised Maria Clara. She is also the sister of Capitan Tiago.
Capitan Tiago He is known for being rich and his sister is Tiya Isabel. He is also kind and willing to help other people.
